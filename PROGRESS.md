- Operations managed by brother (trademark/territory issues being addressed)
- Seller retains full IP and trademark ownership

**Transaction Flexibility:**

### For BC Package Sale (Option 1)
- Brother situation is **not a factor**
- BC operations are completely independent
- Clean title delivered, zero complications

### For Western Canada Package Sale (Option 2)
- Seller will deliver clean title to all territories (BC + AB + SK + MB)
- Transition plan includes:
  - Brother exits Canada operations cleanly
  - All customer relationships transferred to buyer
  - Brother receives Washington State territory setup (funded from proceeds)
  - 90/10 revenue split on USA operations

  - Seller retains IP ownership

**Buyer Position:**
- Choose BC only, or full Western Canada
- Seller handles all operational transitions as part of closing
- No buyer involvement in family negotiations
- Clean delivery guaranteed for either option

**Key Point:** This is a transaction detail, not a risk. Seller controls IP, platform, and customer relationships. Brother transition handled professionally regardless of package size.
